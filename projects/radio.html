About This Mod Pack

(Originally known as Zello Sounds Add-on)

Radio Sounds is a Project That adds realistic radio sounds to the Zello PTT Wilkie Talkie App, nowadays, its mostly for TeamSpeak as Zello is deprecating their desktop app for free users.

Installation Instructions

Zello Legacy PC

Extract to C:\Program Files (x86)\Zello\Snd and open Zello then go to Tools>Options>Sound Alerts.

Set the following:

Incoming message alert: Kenwood SBR IMA.wav or Kenwood NX5800 SBR IMA.wav
Outgoing message alert: Kenwood SBR OMEA.wav
Clear to send alert: Kenwood NX800-5800 SBR C2SA.wav or Motorola SBR C2SA.wav
Incoming message ended alert: Kenwood SBR IMEA.wav or Motorola SBR IMEA.wav

Zello Mobile

Now IDK if you can add these sound on IOS and I can't test it ☠
Extract to Internal Storage/Zello/sounds (older Android) or Internal Storage/Android/data/com.loudtalks/files/sounds (newer Android)
(btw you may need to just need to plug your phone into a pc to find these locations)
and open Zello then go to Settings>Notifcations.

Set the following:

Incoming message: Kenwood SBR IMA.wav or Kenwood NX5800 SBR IMA.wav
Talk button up: Kenwood SBR OMEA.wav
Ready to send: Kenwood NX800-5800 SBR C2SA.wav or Motorola SBR C2SA.wav
End of Incoming message: Kenwood SBR IMEA.wav or Motorola SBR IMEA.wav
