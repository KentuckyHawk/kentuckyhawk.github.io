Community Guidelines

General Conduct:

  • Do not harass, be rude, offensive, or otherwise discriminatory to other Members.
  • Do not spam.
  • NSFW content is Not allowed.
  • Respect other people's points of view.
  • The following topics are not allowed on the server in any way, shape, or form unless explicitly noted otherwise. (Politics, or anything illegal).
  • Profiles must be appropriate and not contain profanity, hate symbols, NSFW Content, or impersonate staff/other community members
  • Avoid excessive special characters or numbers that make names unreadable.

Staff:

  • Staff Will Not monitor, deal with, or make disciplinary actions due to drama or conversations that occur outside the server.
  • Don't backseat moderate.
  • Moderators and admins have the final say in disputes. Follow and respect the instructions of the moderators and administrators.

Etiquette:

  • Treat everyone with respect. None of us care how much you hate that person. If you don’t get along with them, don’t respond to them or bait them.
  • Do not share personal data (yours or others’) without explicit consent.
  • Screenshots of private conversations should not be posted without explicit consent.
  • Each channel has a brief description at the top. Post only content that matches the channel’s purpose (e.g., no CMD’s in the ⁠⁠💬┃general channel).
  • Don't send random Friend/DM requests. ask first...
  • Drama of any sort, as determined by Staff, will be dealt with swiftly and quietly with no grandstanding.

Language:

  • Please use English in text channels for easier communication.
  • Avoid excessive profanity. Keep it friendly and suitable for all ages.

Voice Channels:

  • Keep voice volume at a reasonable level.
  • Be mindful of others when in voice channels. Do not interrupt or talk over people.
  • Don't Join and leave to ear-rape someone.
  • Have some decency when using your camera
  • Do not dominate the conversation

Recruitment and Self-Promotion:

  • Do not spam recruitment messages.
  • Self‑promotion, server invites, or links to external services are only allowed in the #📋┃advertising channel.

Events and Tournaments:

  • Follow any additional event-specific guidelines posted by organizers (e.g., tournaments, role-play settings).
  • Respect the decisions made by event organizers.
